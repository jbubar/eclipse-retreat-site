Why gather for an eclipse at all?

We are not just organizing a trip. We are creating a container for one of the rare moments when the world goes visibly still, the light changes, the temperature drops, and everyone around you feels the same impossible thing at once. A total solar eclipse has a way of rearranging scale. For a few minutes, ordinary time loosens and wonder takes over.

This retreat exists because that kind of moment deserves more than rushing in, snapping a photo, and rushing out. We want to arrive slowly, spend real time together, soak in the Mediterranean coast, and meet totality with enough presence to actually feel it.

Before the sky changes

Beach walks, shared meals, workshops, music, and conversation that let the whole week build toward the main event.

During totality

The sun disappears, the horizon glows, birds quiet down, and the world turns strange and holy for just over a minute.

After it passes

We stay long enough to integrate it: rest, reflection, celebration, and the kind of conversations that only happen after awe.

Cambrils, Spain

A seaside town on the Costa Daurada — relaxed, beautiful, and close to Barcelona. Perfect for a boutique gathering: ocean air, walkable streets, and sunset dinners.

  • Nearest major airport: Barcelona (BCN)
  • Distance: ~1–1.5 hours by car/train (varies)
