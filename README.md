# Solar Eclipse of the Heart

**A boutique, all-inclusive total solar eclipse retreat on the Mediterranean coast.**

**Aug 11–16 • Cambrils, Spain (near Barcelona)**

Live site: [jbubar.github.io/eclipse-retreat-site](https://jbubar.github.io/eclipse-retreat-site/)

---

## About

Solar Eclipse of the Heart is a small-group retreat gathering 16 people on the Costa Daurada to witness a total solar eclipse — and let it mean something. Part adventure, part creative container, part cosmic celebration, hosted by Josh + Harry.

- Immersive and intentional — minimal fluff, maximum presence
- Beachside town of Cambrils: walkable streets, Mediterranean air, sunset dinners
- 1–1.5 hours from Barcelona (BCN) by car or train

## What's Included

- All-inclusive accommodations
- Meals + shared table experiences
- Curated excursions & local culture
- Workshops + creative sessions
- Total eclipse viewing plan with safety guidance
- Optional group transport coordination
- Small group container (16 people)
